I received the OCG Förderpreis 2025 from the Austrian Computer Society (OCG).

The award recognizes my master’s thesis, *Man of Steal: Exploring Model Stealing Attacks Against Image Classifiers*, completed at TU Wien under the supervision of Andreas Rauber and co-supervision of Rudolf Mayer.
